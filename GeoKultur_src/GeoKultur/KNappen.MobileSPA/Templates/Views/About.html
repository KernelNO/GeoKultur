GeoKultur is a tool to discover culture around you. It uses the Norvegiana and Europeana culture databases to find cultural information around you.

It was originally developed for the Apps4Norway national coding competition for best use of open data, and it won first place.

GeoKultur is BSD-licensed open source code. Although initially in independent project, version 2 of GeoKultur is now based on the BSD-licensed source code for Kultur- og Naturreise developed by the Arts Council of Norway.
